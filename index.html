📁 Project

Title: A Cheminformatics Approach for PROTAC-Mediated Clearance of Parkinson’s Disease Protein Aggregates

This project applies cheminformatics to design PROTACs targeting α-synuclein aggregates in Parkinson’s disease. Using fragment-based design, virtual screening, DFT calculations, and docking, two PROTACs were evaluated. The FBDD-derived PROTAC showed better binding affinity and therapeutic potential.
